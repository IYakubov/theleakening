About The Leakening

The Leakening is a gamified task management app designed to help you complete your tasks before they sink — literally!

In this app, every task you create launches a ship across the screen. But beware: as time passes, the ship starts to sink. Your goal? Finish your tasks before the ship goes under!

🚀 How it Works:

  • 📝 You create a task and set a time limit
  • 🚢 A ship appears on the ocean and starts slowly sinking
  • ⏳ The more time passes, the deeper the ship sinks
  • ✅ When you complete a task, press the red “Done” button to turn it green
  • ✅ After completing all tasks, press “Finish Tasks” to save your ship
  • ❌ If you don’t finish on time, the ship sinks completely

We wanted task management to be visual, motivating, and a little bit fun. Instead of boring lists, you get a feeling of urgency and satisfaction — because every task saved is a ship rescued.
